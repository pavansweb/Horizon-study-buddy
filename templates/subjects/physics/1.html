Introduction to Physics

Physics is the natural science that studies matter, its motion and behavior through space and time, and the related entities of energy and force. Physics is one of the most fundamental scientific disciplines, and its main goal is to understand how the universe behaves.

Key Concepts

  • Matter and Energy
  • Forces and Motion
  • Space and Time
  • Fundamental Particles

Branches of Physics

  1. Classical Mechanics
  2. Thermodynamics
  3. Electromagnetism
  4. Quantum Mechanics
  5. Relativity

This chapter will provide an overview of these concepts and branches, setting the foundation for your journey into the fascinating world of physics.
